Working through the Pipeline

Within EVA youll find 4 pipeline stages: Match,Screen ,Meet and Place.

Screen compiles all incoming job applications and profiles found in Match and enables you to review, make decisions and contact your matched Candidates with ease against the Job you are recruiting for.

Meet is designed to review all matched and screened Candidates that are in Interview stage as part of the recruitment process.

Place allows you to manage and close all aspects of your newly made Placement following completion of the recruitment process. Read the articles above to find out more.
